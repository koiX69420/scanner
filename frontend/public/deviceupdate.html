Update your device to use the Mandog Trench Tools Chrome Extension on

This is an automated process. If your connection is slow, this may take a bit longer

  1. You need to sign a transaction to proof you are the owner of the wallet, given that you are already verified
  2. No further payments are necessary for the device change.
  3. Clicking on the Mandog extension will now show a field to enter a Solana CA (Contract Address). It will also be integrated as a clickable icon on Photon, Bullx and Axiom (Token Page).
  4. You are now ready to begin scanning. Good luck and happy trenching!
